About

I've created this section to briefly discuss what software and hardware I use, and to showcase a collection of some of the projects I have created with said software/hardware.

Blender

Blender has extensive functions for modeling, sculpting, baking, texture painting, material creation, rigging and animating, and rendering. This, coupled with the fact that it is free and open source, has made Blender my go to program for any 3D graphics work for the past few years. See Blender Images.

Adobe Photoshop/Adobe Illustrator

Pretty much required for just about any graphics design media. I've used both Photoshop and Illustrator for retouching photos and creating wallpapers, textures, and logos. See Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator Images.

Illustration

I've used drawing tablets for both college courses and personal projects. I currently use Wacom drawing tablets to create illustrations in both Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator. See Digital Illustrations.
