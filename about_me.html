About Me

Ever since I can remember, I've enjoyed illustration. In my early teen years, I was introduced to video game development, where I found my passion in both 2D and 3D digital graphics design. Since then, I've honed my skills in art and graphics design through practice, education, and the use of various mediums and programs.

I graduated from Clearwater High School and obtained an Associate of Arts degree from Saint Petersburg College. While in high school, I earned Adobe Certified Associate certifications in both Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator, and proceeded to take multiple graphic design courses using Adobe programs in college. During my spare time, I worked on multiple personal projects creating 3D art using Blender, Substance 3D Painter, and Photoshop.

My name is Paul Santoro and I currently live in Clearwater Florida. If you wish to contact me, you can by email at santorop16@gmail.com.
