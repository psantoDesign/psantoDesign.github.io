Stone Arch Asset

Previous Next

I wanted to challenge myself by creating a simple 3D asset for the Unity game engine from scratch in the shortest amount of time. Once I decided that I wanted to make a stone archway, I opened Blender and started sculpting away. When my high poly sculpt was complete, I created a low poly mesh over the high poly sculpt, making sure to keep the geometry clean so that I could easily edit it later. Since I had decided to create this asset completely from scratch and still needed to make a material, I quickly took some pictures of my driveway pavement and imported them into Photoshop. Once I turned my pavement into usable texture resources, I imported them, along with the 3D model I created, into Substance Painter to create the material. After finishing editing the material in Substance Painter, I went back to Blender and made a few levels of detail for the low poly mesh. I then imported all the resources into a new Unity project and once I completed setting up the material and LOD settings for the archway, I was done. In total this project took about 6 hours to complete.
