Happy Bot Animation

Previous Next

I was inspired to create this little fellow one night after watching a tutorial about how to "shuffle dance" while browsing videos on YouTube. I started modeling away in Blender, and while animating, I listened to some upbeat music. The result: a playful rendition of a robot with a giant grin on its face and a seemingly friendly character.
